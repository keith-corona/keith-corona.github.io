About

As a child, I was always attracted to the challenges - the things people said I couldn't do or couldn't achieve. Also as a child, I was often saddened because I wanted hundreds of lives to live so that I could experience an entire lifetime not just a glimpse into the life of a fisherman, a sailor, a soldier, a writer, an artist, a farmer, or whatever else.



In a compromise of taking the challenge of living a hundred authentic lives, I have subconciously led a life that could be interpreted as without direction, but in reality, it has been a life led by the direction of experience. And in experience, I am rich.



I am on a path to another change by segueing into web development. I am hoping that this path will allow me to express my creativity in a functional way - mixing my love of art and design with my obsession for purpose.



And so, here is my introduction. My work draws influence from my travels, my childhood, my adulthood, and my experiences. I am an aspiring web developer who enjoys front-end work at the moment, though I think some back-end languages seem pretty interesting and worth learning in the future.



If anything here interests you, or you have a question for me, please reach out.



-Keith
